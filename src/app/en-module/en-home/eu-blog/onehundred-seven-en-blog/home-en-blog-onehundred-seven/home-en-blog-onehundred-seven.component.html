The Methodology Running Through Every Publication

Every one of the 242 ideas applies a consistent analytical framework built on three pillars:

Wyckoff Method and Volume Analysis: Not rigid schematics, but reading institutional intent through accumulation, markup, distribution, and markdown phases as they unfold in real time. Divergences between price movement and volume are consistently sought to identify institutional behavior before it becomes visible on the price chart alone.

Smart Money Concepts: Tracking institutional participants through volume footprints, liquidity analysis, and positioning data — smart money, whales, large participants, hedgers.

Cross-Market Correlation Analysis: Connecting dollar index, gold, crude oil, stock indices, and crypto into a unified macro picture. This multi-asset approach produced the most accurate calls, including the 2022 crypto winter thesis that earned the Editor's Pick.
