Smart Money - trading strategy

Hello everyone! Today I want to talk about Smart Money.

Who are Smart Money?

Smart Money are the intelligent money that manipulates the market. Our task as traders is to know about them, their techniques, see their actions, and respond accordingly to also make a profit. Smart Money act rationally: they sell high and buy low. When they are active, high volumes are always visible. That is why volume analysis is so important — it shows where the smart money is active. Where they are active, the price will soon change, and this can be used to your advantage.

How do Smart Money operate?

Large money is limited in liquidity, so they use tricks. For example, where they need to sell, they create demand. How? Most traders react to levels and place their stop-losses behind them. Those who sell place a stop-loss behind the nearest level, and those who work on the breakout place buy orders. When the price rises above the level, sellers close losses and buy back, while those working on the breakout buy. This creates a large pool of buy liquidity that Smart Money use. They create demand by breaking the level and actively selling into it. As a result, the exchange registers a huge volume, and after a few small drops, we see that the volume drops sharply — this is a signal that they have finished selling. The market can only fall down.

Conclusion

To effectively use smart money, gather liquidity at levels. At low levels, they buy, at high levels, they sell. Many traders look at levels just like Smart Money, so the latter manipulates the price to create favorable conditions for themselves. These are local manipulations but important. When we see a lack of demand at a high price level, it means that Smart Money were selling. This also works at low levels: if the volume decreases on the decline, it means the professionals have already bought, and the market is ready to turn around.
