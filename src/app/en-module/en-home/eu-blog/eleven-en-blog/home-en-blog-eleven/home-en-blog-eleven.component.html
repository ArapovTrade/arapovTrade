Trader's starting deposit

Hello everyone! My name is Igor Arapov, and today I want to share important information about the starting deposit with beginner traders. Over 11 years of trading, I have seen many situations, especially in the cryptocurrency market, where beginners get lost due to a lack of understanding of this field. In this post, I will try to explain in more detail how to start correctly and avoid mistakes.

Your Deposit is Your Business

It is important to understand that your deposit is your business. Everything you earn or lose is part of your work for yourself. Many beginners do not achieve success on the first try, and that is normal. Just like in any other business, it takes time to understand and become a professional in trading.

The foundation of success in trading is understanding the market and knowing how to trade correctly. It does not matter what size your deposit is if you do not know when to buy and when to sell. Many beginners try to buy low and sell high but often do the opposite, leading to losses.

For experienced traders, the size of the deposit is important because it directly affects profitability. The average conservative profitability ranges from 2 to 5% per month. However, these rules do not apply to beginners. Even with a large deposit, if you do not understand how to work in the market, you risk losing your funds.

Advice for Beginner Traders

My advice for beginner traders is not to focus immediately on the size of the deposit. Start with a small amount, make systematic and thoughtful trades, and only then draw conclusions about what deposit size you need.

Remember that in trading, it is important not only to earn but also to manage risks. This is a business, not a game of chance. Gradually study trading, gain experience with small accounts, and when you are confident in your abilities, then you can consider increasing your deposit.
