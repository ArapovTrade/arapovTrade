What is Smart Money?

Smart Money are large financial institutions that play a significant role in the market. They buy when prices are low, often visible by huge trading volumes. When prices are high, they tend to sell, which is also reflected in their trading volumes. For a retail trader, it is very beneficial to learn to recognize the levels that interest these large players and be able to align with their strategies.
