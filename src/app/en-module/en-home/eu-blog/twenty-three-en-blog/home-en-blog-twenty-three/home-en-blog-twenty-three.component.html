Liquidity in Trading

Hello everyone! My name is Igor Arapov. Today I will tell you about levels and the concept of Smart Money. Levels show value, and everything you want to trade using volume analysis needs to be found at levels. Big money and banks always buy low and sell high, and we see this through volumes. Therefore, it is important to be able to identify these levels and use volume analysis to see when Smart Money is active in the market and to use this information to your advantage.

Actions of Smart Money

Smart Money always buys when the market is falling. They increase the supply to buy. For example, when the price falls below the minimum level, it knocks out buyers and activates stop-losses. Traders who trade on a level breakout enter short positions, which allows professionals to place limit orders and accumulate positions.

Accumulation Phases

How to determine the beginning and end of the accumulation phase? The beginning of the phase is very high volume when they are actively buying. The end of the phase is when bearish liquidity runs out, and they have no choice but to buy at the market. At this point, there is no liquidity, and the price begins to rise.

Market Direction Change

When you see low volume in a high volume zone, be prepared for the market to change direction. These situations often occur on news because news explains why the market is moving. If the news is bad, expect a local trend until professionals reach their goal and lock in profits. They sell just as they bought: buying from someone and selling to someone. Buying from the crowd at lows and selling at highs, they make a profit, while uninformed participants incur losses.

Conclusion

Levels are a key tool in technical analysis. There are always manipulations at levels aimed at misleading uninformed participants, knocking out stop-losses, and then developing a trend. Experienced traders wait for such situations at levels and then join the new movement.
