How to make money from trading?

Hello! My name is Igor Arapov, and today I will talk about how to make money in trading. For many beginners, this is an important question, and I will try to explain what traders earn on.

Leverage

I want to start with the concept of “Leverage”. Leverage, or margin, is a tool that allows traders to increase the volume of their trades using borrowed funds. This means that a trader can open a position that significantly exceeds the size of their own capital.

It is important to note that many companies provide leverage to their traders. This feature allows trading with a larger volume while having fewer funds, but leverage creates a significant risk for your deposit if you do not know how to use it correctly. Overestimating risk in trading is prohibited because you can lose money if you do not handle market analysis properly.

From this logic follows: you need to learn to analyze the market so that when buying gold, the price actually goes up and not the other way around; otherwise, you will incur a loss. To prevent this, you need to study technical market analysis, volume market analysis, the Smart Money concept, levels — these are all analytical tools used by traders.

Once you master these types of analysis, you can start trading. Your task is to predict the price. Simply buying gold and forgetting about it will not work because you are not an investor, and price changes can significantly affect your deposit, especially if you trade with leverage. I already mentioned that trading with leverage carries risk, and this risk needs to be controlled. For this, stop-losses are used — orders that limit risk. In trading, you cannot be a passive observer; you need to actively trade, control trades, and quickly change your opinion about the market.

The profession of a trader requires always being on alert and adapting to market changes while controlling risk. Pay attention to profitability in this business. Trading is a high-yield business, but only if you know how to handle it correctly.
