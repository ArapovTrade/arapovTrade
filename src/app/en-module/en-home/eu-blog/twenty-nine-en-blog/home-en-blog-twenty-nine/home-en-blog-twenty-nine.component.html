What do you need to know to start quickly?

I recommend getting acquainted with support and resistance levels right away. This is a full course, but there are specific lessons on levels. You need to get acquainted with bounces and breakouts of levels. These two topics are key. If you master them — it's literally a few lessons — you can already apply them. Starting on a demo account, you will see that it works, and you can gradually implement this on your real deposit. Just two topics: level breakout and level bounce. Nothing else is needed to start trading.

All other topics complement each other to create a complete picture of trading, improve your entry points, add varieties of these points, and make you more effective. But for a start, you only need level breakout and level bounce. If you learn these two topics, you can already start, and trading will not seem difficult. Most importantly, it will not be exhausting because you can learn trading endlessly. People study for years and do not achieve results. You can know everything about trading but not be a trader because all trading is about the ability to buy low and sell high. This is a skill, not theoretical knowledge. To develop this skill, you need to learn methods to develop it. By working with levels, you develop the skill of buying low and selling high. In a month or two, you will understand that trading is not as difficult as it seemed at the beginning, and you will confidently develop.
