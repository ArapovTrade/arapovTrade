The main reasons for losing a deposit

Hello, friends! I'm Igor Arapov, and I have been trading for over 11 years. Today, I want to share my experience with beginners and discuss the main reasons why traders lose their deposits. These are the key mistakes that lead to a complete loss of money.

First and foremost

Deposit loss occurs due to a lack of understanding of the profession. If a trader does not understand what they are doing in the market, they are doomed to lose money. This manifests in several ways: firstly, they do not control risks. Without risk control, your deposit will be lost – it is only a matter of time, because most traders trade with leverage. We are not talking about investing, where you buy something and forget about it, but about leveraged trading, where margin creates conditions in which, if the price goes against you by a sufficient number of points, you will simply be stopped out and lose everything.

Lack of risk control is the first and main reason for account loss. Many beginners do not understand where to place a stop-loss. They see the price moving up and down and think: why place a stop-loss when you can just hold the trade in the market? You need to wait, and the price will go in the desired direction, and I will break even or make a profit.

This approach is dangerous. There are phases when the market moves sideways, and within this range, you can wait out any market movement. But remember the traders who tried to short Bitcoin throughout its entire rise. The CME exchange showed how many millions and billions were lost by short-traders, that is, those who tried to sell Bitcoin futures. A future works in such a way that you take Bitcoin as collateral and are obliged to return it regardless of the price difference. If you sell Bitcoin and it rises, you will have to buy it back at higher prices, fixing a loss.

On this entire trend, a huge amount of money was lost. The market simply soared without a chance of a return, and those who sold either lost their deposits or are on the verge of it. The percentage change in the asset was so significant that no proper deposit management could have saved it.

If you approach trading this way, profitability will tend to zero, and it simply does not make sense. The history of Bitcoin showed that without risk control, you are doomed to lose your account – this is the first main problem.

The second problem

Is risk overestimation. Even if you place stops, you still try to earn much more than realistically possible, and you overestimate the lot size, for example, up to 5, 10, 15% of the account. You think that everything will go now, and you will make a lot of money. But in the long run, this leads to failure because there are no strategies that with a 99% probability always provide profitable entry points. A normal strategy gives 70 to 30: out of 100 trades, 30 will be losing. It can be a big loss or a small one, it all depends on the setting of your trading system. But with a probability of 70 to 30, in thirty cases, you will be at a loss. If you remember about dispersion in mathematics, it turns out that 30 losing trades can fall in a row 5, 6, 7, 8, even 10 times. The chance will decrease, but it is real. Ten times you get a loss in a row, and dispersion simply eats your deposit.

Everything else is not so critical and not so dangerous for the deposit as for both beginners and experienced traders. The trader themselves is usually the cause of the deposit loss, not the market. The market lives its life. Fighting the market is the problem. The trader who sells the trend is the problem, not the market.

To summarize

  • First – lack of risk control. Without stops, you are doomed.
  • Second – overestimated lot, that is, overestimated trade volume.
  • Third – arguing with the market.

If you eliminate these three main problems from your trading, most likely, everything will be fine with you. In my 11 years, I have seen that these were always the key factors. No matter how many traders I have communicated with, no matter how many bitter mistakes I made at the beginning, these three problems were always at the core. If you get rid of them, you can safely trade, develop your account, and everything will be fine with you. But if you start making these mistakes, be prepared for problems – they will not keep you waiting long.
