Rules for Successful Trading

Hello! My name is Igor Arapov and today we will discuss the basic rules that will help you improve your trading.

So, three basic rules that should not be broken: do not trade against the trend, do not risk too much in one trade, and always use stop-losses to control risks. By breaking these rules, you risk losing your deposit over time. These principles are very important for the safety of your funds on the exchange. If you are interested in learning more, watch my previous videos.

Now about the good rules that help gain an edge in the market. The whole secret of a trader's work lies in using all possible factors that give you an advantage over the market and applying them in your trades. This makes your mathematical expectation positive, meaning you consistently make money in the long run.

The first good rule is not to get stuck with one instrument. Many traders focus only on one instrument, which can lead to drawdowns. It is recommended to create a portfolio of several key instruments and trade them when there are good entry points. This helps avoid problems associated with dependence on one market and allows you to choose the best opportunities.

The second important rule is understanding the role of big capital and “Smart Money”. Do not trade against the trend, as the trend is determined by market liquidity. “Smart Money” tries to enter the market and move the price in their favor using volumes and liquidity. Understanding this helps you identify entry and exit points in the market based on volume analysis.

I hope these simple rules will help you improve your trading.
