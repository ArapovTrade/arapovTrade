Top myths about Trading

Hello, friends! My name is Igor Arapov and today we will talk about myths in trading.

Myth One — High Profitability

Many beginners start with cryptocurrencies, thinking they will get rich quickly. When Bitcoin is rising, it seems easy. But the cryptocurrency market is young and unstable. Experienced traders earn from 2 to 5% per month, and 5% is a very good result. All stories about 100%, 200%, and higher profitability are myths. Yes, sometimes you can make a lucky investment and earn, but stable income is more important. It's better to have small but consistent earnings than a one-time big win. Participants who are prone to adventure try to guess which coin will shoot up to cover losses from all the others, but this is not classic trading.

Myth Two — Trading is Difficult

Trading is not so much difficult as it requires self-control. It's similar to gambling, and many lose money due to greed and the desire to make quick profits. The main difficulty is controlling your emotions. Trading is specific but not as difficult as higher mathematics. Some beginners think that they will watch a few videos and immediately become pros. This is a mistaken belief. To be a successful trader, you need to know the right things and stick to a system.

Myth Three — Trading is Confused with Investing

Investing is owning an asset that generates profit. Trading is not investing. When you buy coins, you are trying to guess their growth, not investing. Real investments are not related to speculation.

If you buy a factory, it should work for you, and you should only be concerned about the size of the dividend yield, not the market value of your factory.
