Smart Money

Hello! My name is Igor Arapov. Today we will continue discussing the Smart Money concept. In previous posts, we looked at examples where Smart Money was buying assets. Today, we will look at a case where Smart Money was selling.

Smart Money often manipulates the market to shake out participants and gather liquidity. When we analyze volumes, it is important to see if this volume touches any level.

If you look at the chart in my video https://youtu.be/oT8aVcWI2Oo?si=XlqMByAIzhVQ_sul, you can see that the price was rising. I was trading at that time and remember that there was important news.

Tip

Pay attention to key news. It always triggers high activity in the market, which is visible in volumes. News usually changes the perception of value in the market, affecting price and volumes.

According to Smart Money logic, to sell, you need to do it where there is no demand. A market drop is always associated with a drop in demand. In our example, in the zone with high volumes during the news, there was a false rise, after which the price fell. The next four-hour bar shows that there is no support in the market, and Smart Money is not interested. This is the beginning of the distribution phase; then, the distribution phase ends, and the trend phase begins.

This is an example according to Smart Money logic, but for selling. The principles are the same: a zone with liquidity, its absence, and a trend change.
