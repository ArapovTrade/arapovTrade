How the Videos Connect to the Published Books

The lessons on the @ArapovTrade channel are not separate from the published books. They represent the same educational system delivered in video format:

"Trading Fundamentals" (Vol. 1) — ISBN 979–8–90243–075–9 (RU)
→ Category 6 (Beginners) + Category 4 (Technical Analysis): market basics, levels, candlestick patterns, support and resistance

"Trading Fundamentals Vol. 2" — ISBN 979–8–90243–078–0 (RU)
→ Category 2 (Wyckoff and Volume) + Category 3 (Smart Money): volume analysis, Wyckoff phases, institutional behavior, order flow

"Trading Psychology" — ISBN 979–8–90243–081–0 (RU)
→ Category 5 (Psychology and Risk): emotional control, drawdown management, stop-loss discipline, mathematical expectancy

All three books are available in Russian, Ukrainian, and English — each with its own ISBN (Bowker USA) and DOI (CERN/Zenodo). All three Ukrainian editions are held in the collection of the Vernadsky National Library of Ukraine, cataloged as the "Osnovy treydinhu" series.
