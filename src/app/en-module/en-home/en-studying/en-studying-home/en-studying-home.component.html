Want to become an experienced trader?
The foundation of successful trading is always a trading system with a positive mathematical expectation (or positive expectancy), and nothing else. All profitable traders are united by the presence of such systems.

The profitability of traders differs depending on the capital management formulas used. Since there are a large number of these formulas, the distribution of profitability ranges from conservative to high-risk approaches.

Experienced traders never inflate risks in trading. Instead, they balance positive mathematical expectation with risk control in an individual trade to achieve a positive result over a series of trades (or in the long run).
