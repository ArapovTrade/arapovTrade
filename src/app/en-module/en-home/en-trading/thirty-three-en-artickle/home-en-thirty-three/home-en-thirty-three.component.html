Requotes

In dealing with dealing centers (DC) that provide FOREX quotes through the Instant Execution system, a requote is an offer to repeat the request for the current quote. This occurs if you try to place a market order at the last price, but for some reason, that price has already become outdated (due to slow internet connection or high volatility). In other words, your market order gets rejected.

On the stock market, requotes do not exist (but an order may fail to reach the broker or the exchange due to an internet connection breakdown or any other technical issues).
