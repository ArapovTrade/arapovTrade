How to make money trading FOREX

To earn money in the Forex market, you need to understand the following: to make money on buying and subsequently selling any currency, you buy it at a lower price and sell it at a higher price, earning on the difference between these prices.

For example, if you buy 15,000 pesos from a bank for 1,000 dollars, and then when the exchange rate rises to 29,000, you sell them, your profit will be 14,000 pesos.

However, many people face two problems.

  • such transactions require a significant amount of money
  • waiting for the exchange rate to change can take a lot of time

In the Forex market, the situation is different.

First, you can carry out trading operations on amounts that exceed your own funds by 100-200 times, using leverage of 1:100 or 1:200, respectively. In other words, by funding an account with 100 dollars, you can trade with an amount of 10,000-20,000 dollars.

Where does this money come from? The broker provides it for currency purchase (of course, not for free, they keep the spread from each trading operation).

Second, exchange rates in the Forex market are constantly changing under the influence of millions of transactions carried out by banks, corporations, private investors, and other market participants every minute.

Third, in Forex, you do not have to wait for a significant change in the exchange rate. You can earn even on small price fluctuations of 10-20 pips (0.001-0.002 units). For example, by buying 10,000 euros (0.1 lot) at a price of 1.2745 and selling them at a price of 1.2765 in 20 minutes, you can earn 20 dollars.

If you invest 1,000 dollars and buy 100,000 euros, then under the same conditions, you will earn 200 dollars. This is a profit, despite the fact that the price changed by only 20 pips (0.002 units) in just 20 minutes. Daily price fluctuations in Forex can reach up to 200 pips and even more.

Of course, you can also earn money by selling currency. In this case, you do not need to buy the currency first; you simply open a sell position, and the bank automatically sells the currency from its reserves. Then, when the exchange rate drops, you close the position, and the difference between the sale and purchase will be your profit.

In general, to earn money in Forex, you need to buy, expecting the exchange rate to rise, and sell, expecting it to fall. If your assumptions are correct and the currency price changes accordingly, you can earn on the difference in currency price.
