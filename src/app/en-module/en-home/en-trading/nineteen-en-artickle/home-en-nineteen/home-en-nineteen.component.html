How to Trade on the FOREX Currency Market Online

To start trading on the currency market today, you need a reliable broker, a computer, and Internet access. So, to start trading, you need to sign a contract with a broker (dealing center) for the provision of services. A reliable broker provides good trading conditions (the ability to open a demo or cent account with a minimal deposit, low spread, no commission, leverage from 1:1 to 1:500, fast execution of trading orders, a large number of instruments — currency pairs for trading).

HOW TO OPEN A TRADING ACCOUNT

  • Register on the broker's website
  • Fill out a trader's questionnaire, indicating your name, email, set a password for your personal account and trading terminal
  • Choose which account to open: demo account (virtual money trading on the real market), cent account (trading real money with a minimal deposit of 1 USD) or a regular trading account with a deposit of 100 USD. The choice of account largely determines the trading conditions and potential profit.
  • Select the leverage size: 1:1 (trading only with your own funds, without involving broker funds, a large deposit of 10,000 USD is required), 1:10 (one to ten), the ability to operate an amount 10 times larger than the deposit size when trading; 1:100 (the available amount when trading will be 100 times the deposit size, this is the most optimal ratio for starting), 1:200 or 1:500.
  • After this, an email will be sent with a link to complete the registration and open an account.
  • Open a personal account (using the login and password specified in the personal account questionnaire), here you will be able to download the trading terminal, and all trading data will be specified (account number — also the login for the terminal, server for connection, the password was specified in the questionnaire).
  • Through the personal account, top up the trading account and withdraw profits in any convenient way.
  • Download the trading terminal and install it on the computer.
  • Launch the trading terminal, specifying the account number as the login, the password specified in the questionnaire for the trading terminal, and the server for connection with the FOREX market.

Now you can start trading CFD contracts on currency.

To understand the terminal, learn how to open and close positions, select working tools, choose the right lot sizes, and try your skills in practice, it is recommended to start with a demo account. A cent account will allow you to try trading real money with a small deposit (from 1 USD). A trading account will allow you to trade and earn real money trading currency.

It is worth remembering

Trading on the currency market is a difficult and stressful job that should be approached with all seriousness and responsibility. To make trading on Forex a source of constant and stable income, it is not enough to just understand the trading terminal, you need to constantly learn, not only in training courses and books but also through your own experience. You need to accumulate knowledge and skills, studying new trading strategies, developing your own trading strategy or system, and constantly improving, supplementing, and perfecting it.

To start, it is worth studying the basics of technical and fundamental analysis; this will allow you to understand what affects the price, learn to read charts, and make accurate forecasts of future price movements. It is also worth studying theories of capital management and the psychological aspects of trading on the exchange; this will significantly reduce the risk of transactions and make decisions based on rational analysis rather than emotional outbursts. Constant development and learning will allow you to earn steadily and a lot in the future.
