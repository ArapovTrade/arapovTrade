Profit and Loss Ratio

A successful trading system consists of two parts:

  • limiting losses
  • timely profit-taking

Sometimes traders' strategies involve a strict ratio of stop and take profit positions, for example, 1 to 3. Thus, a stop of 10 points will correspond to a take profit of 30.

At the same time, it is necessary to remember that in such a case the take profit must exceed the stop, as a rare strategy allows the trader to trade without losses, and short stops imply that, for example, two losing trades can compensate for one profitable one.
