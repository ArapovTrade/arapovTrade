FOREX Market

FOREX (FOREX) is a place where national currencies are freely bought and sold based on the exchange rates between them (quotes), formed in the market under the influence of supply and demand.

Main Purpose of the FOREX Market

The primary purpose of creating the FOREX market was the international exchange of currency. Later, with the advent of margin trading and the increase in the number of dealers, the possibility of speculative operations to make a profit appeared. Currently, there are also commercial companies, multinational corporations, and banks that seek to simply exchange one currency for another without aiming to make a profit from it. Why do they need this? It's very simple: they need to exchange profits earned abroad in foreign currency for the national currency or the currency of a third country where the company needs to pay salaries and cover other expenses. The largest players — central national banks of different countries — do not set any formal restrictions on price movements, but in special cases, they conduct currency interventions (i.e., buy or sell the national currency to prevent further increases or decreases in its exchange rate).

However, this segment accounts for no more than 5% of all FOREX trading turnover. The remaining 95% is provided by the capital of currency traders and organizations whose goal is to make a profit from currency rate movements.
