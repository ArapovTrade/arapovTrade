Trading book list

"Trading Fundamentals" is a book series by Igor Arapov , presenting a comprehensive trader preparation system that spans topics from mathematical foundations and market mechanics to decision-making psychology. The author adapts chess strategy principles and physics-based analytical methods for application in financial markets. The methodology prioritizes the Wyckoff Method and Smart Money concepts.

All three books in the series — "Trading Fundamentals" — are stored in the collection of the V. I. Vernadsky National Library of Ukraine.
